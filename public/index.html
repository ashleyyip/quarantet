The new way to play music, together

Quarantet was developed to enable musicians to come together and make music virtually. Especially when rehearsals are cancelled, Quarantet connects musicians by allowing them to perform as an ensemble even when recording parts in isolation.

Embedded click tracks

Click tracks ensure everyone is playing in sync. It remains constant for the duration of the room.

Share with one button

With unique links, users can easily share their room with others. No login required.
